Sweep paper wallet

You can add more coins to your wallet by sweeping paper wallets. When you sweep a paper wallet, all the coins are moved from the original address and into your current wallet.

After the transfer is complete, you should avoid reusing the paper wallet for security reasons.

Supported formats: WIF, BIP38, Mini private key.
