Auto-Lock
Auto-Lock will automatically lock your account after 5 minutes. That means you won't be able to perform any transactions again, without supplying your personal password.
Clear on exit
You can clear all data on every exit of the application. This require you to enter your 12-word phrases every time you use the app.
